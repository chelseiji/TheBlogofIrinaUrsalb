After
I don't know exactly what went down after we left.

Like the rest of the nation, I've only seen fragments. Grainy footage. Whispers passed from sister to sister.

Things I've heard:

Other mining sectors rose.

People rioted in their Blocs.

A group of Sisters came to the Chambers.

All of Bloc 1 hid inside for their own safety.

They dragged the Director and made him stand trial.

They ....

The footage cuts out, but the last thing you see is the Director sitting peacefully while a group of sisters surrounds him.

I don't know for certain if that monster is dead yet or if he's tried to run.

What I do know is that I finally saw hope in the eyes of people. I saw laughter, I saw tears. I saw people holding up images of him with his eyes crossed out.

They believe he's dead, but I'm not exactly convinced.

—-

But.

A new name has spread. A man from one of the further sectors. His name has been spreading. He, too, was a former general before he was dismissed by The First Director. A man who claims he always knew the Director was behind this. A man with a calm but strong impression.

Reminds me of what they said when the director came to power.

I've expressed my worries to Viktor.

"Your sisters have good hands. The question is whether they'll keep them," he told me.

—-

I received a message tonight, but I do not know from whom.

It reads:

The network is now the government. We did not originally plan for this. We planned simply for resistance. Come to us when you can. We need to discuss further action.

I'm not sure what is going to happen next, or who will take power.

All I know is that things are okay for now. Food is not being rationed any longer. People are laughing and talking to each other again. I have some hope.
